WE ARE CORHIKE

We are the Pune base start up working on knowledge transfer & building relation between Universities & Corporate. During the MBA Studies I Found the need of professional relation with the corporate world which not only increase my professional relation but also give brief about practical concept, challenges faced by employee, competencies need for job which may completely differ from educational learning similarly corporate interaction gives information about organization work culture, management, work responsibilities, general policies etc. which help student to take right decision in their carrier. Similarly, this platform help corporate to find best of best talent from universities & build strong relation between the student & employee.

Our Vision and Mission

To bring professional relation in community.

To bring practical learning into student education similarly brings universities research, knowledge & learnings to corporate.
