WE ARE CORHIKE

We are the Pune base start up working on knowledge transfer & building relation between Universities & Corporate.
Our vision is to bring professional relation in community..

CORHIKE FOR EXPERT

CORHIKE create network of corporate expert to connect with students from Universities & other company employee. CORHIKE is design such a way that expert communicate with other company employee & student without disturbing their personal life by appointment system. It also have review & recommendation system to find competences within them.

FOUNDER OF CORHIKE

ABHILASH ARSOD

FOUNDER

CORHIKE FOR STUDENT

CORHIKE gives platform to University student to increase professional relation with corporate. It help them to increase their field knowledge & to find right carrier path. It aware those about competences need for jobs, challenges, work culture & opportunities. Expert help student to find competences within them by review system.
